About Us

Meet Vivienne Fisher - our founder, our driving force, and our resident slime enthusiast. Her story is one of passion, inspiration, and an unyielding love for the joyful world of slime.

From a young age, Vivienne was captivated by the transformative properties of slime. This seemingly simple substance, which could flow like a liquid, yet be handled like a solid, seemed nothing short of magical. Vivienne would spend countless hours mixing and playing, entranced by the vibrant colors, the soothing textures, and the infinite potential that slime offered.

But what started as a childhood fascination soon evolved into something more profound. Vivienne realized that slime was more than just a plaything. It was a medium for creative expression, a tool for sensory development, and a source of unadulterated joy. She wanted to share this unique and whimsical world with others - and so, her journey into entrepreneurship began.

Vivienne's commitment to slime goes beyond its playful appeal. She understands its potential as a therapeutic tool, its ability to soothe the mind, and bring people into the moment. It's this vision that guides our company - a belief in the power of simple, tactile joy to make a difference in our busy, digital-first world.

Today, Vivienne sells slime not just as a product, but as an experience - a small, colorful escape from the rigors of everyday life. Each tub of slime we sell is a testament to her passion, bringing a slice of joy and a dash of wonder to customers worldwide.

But the story doesn't end here. Just as slime continues to shift and change, so too does Vivienne's ambition. She is continuously dreaming, inventing, and refining - all with the goal of delivering the best slime experience possible.

Because to Vivienne Fisher, slime is more than just a product. It's a promise of joy, a commitment to quality, and an invitation to embrace your playful side. And she invites you to join her on this colorful journey.

Welcome to our world of slime - where play meets passion, and joy is just a squeeze away.
